About Us

Our strategic presence on both sides of the Diaspora created a unique opportunity that has allowed us to cultivate a niche market by providing customized solutions and unparalleled logistics support services in both North America and Africa, thus giving us an edge over the competition.

As the market leader in our field, our clients rely on our expertise for Travel, Meetings, Trade Delegations and other logistical support needs. We provide full planning and facilitation and support services for all events including conferences, meetings, seminars and workshops facilitation.

Our team of hand-picked experts in all aspects of the travel and meetings facilitation, your designated trip or event manager will see to every precise detail of your program. In addition to individuals and Families, our clientele includes Corporations seeking to host customer and staff targeted events, Business & Trade Associations, Governments and Non Profit Organizations hosting Trade Delegations, Conventions and Trade Shows.

Tropical Destinations

Bali, Indonesia

There is no other place like Bali in this world. The Island of the Gods offers great beaches, countless waves for surfing and wonderful natural sites to visit and explore, colorful ceremonies, and gifted artists. There is an unbelievably wide range of hotels, resorts and villas, restaurants & bars, spas and not to forget:world-class activities and shopping in Bali - all within close distance and at very fair prices. Bali is famous for a very high value-for-money-ratio, which allows traveler to enjoy and afford the wonder of this island, regardless of their budget. Choose from basic home-stays run by friendly Balinese families, and 5star beach resorts or luxurious, or privately-serviced Bali villas with your private pool.

Asia Destination

Welcome to the Maldives, where sands are white as the smiles of the locals, where fish swim happily in the warm waters of the Indian Ocean, where the weather is a dream, and the deep rays of the sun wait to engulf you in their arms.

Carribean Destination

Bahamas

Renowned as a maritime playground for sun-starved Americans, this stunning string of subtropical islands is so much more than a cruise-ship stopover. Stretched between the depths of the North Atlantic and Florida's eastern coast, the Bahamas comprises more than 700 islands and 2400 cays, most uninhabited, and all fringed by spectacular coral and fathomless ocean trenches.
